Jalapeno Popper Cups Recipe

A creamy and spicy appetizer using Jalapenos.

Ingredients

  • 12 mini phyllo tart shells
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • Â½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
  • bacon bits

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place phyllo cups onto a baking sheet.
  2. Stir together cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, jalapenos, and hot sauce in a bowl. Spoon mixture into phyllo cups. Sprinkle bacon bits on top.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes. Serve warm.
